Red Rocks Mother's Day Brunch 2023: A Family-Friendly Outdoor Dining Experience

Looking for a unique way to celebrate Mother’s Day in 2023?

Join us at Red Rocks for a delicious brunch in a stunning outdoor setting!

Our restaurant has been a beloved local institution for over a decade, and we’re excited to welcome families for a special Mother’s Day celebration in 2023. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing morning spent in nature or a chance to indulge in some of the best food in town, our brunch has something for everyone.

Here are just a few of the highlights you can look forward to:

Delicious Food and Drinks

Our chefs have put together a special menu just for Mother’s Day, featuring classic brunch favorites with a unique twist. From our famous avocado toast to fluffy pancakes with fresh berries and cream, there’s something to satisfy every palate. And don’t forget to raise a glass of our signature mimosa or Bloody Mary to toast to the special mom in your life!

Breathtaking Scenery

Red Rocks is known for its stunning natural beauty, and our outdoor dining area offers unparalleled views of the surrounding mountains and red rock formations. Take a stroll through our gardens before or after your meal to soak up the scenery and snap some photos with Mom.

Live Music

We know that no celebration is complete without a little music, so we’ve lined up some of the best local musicians to provide a soundtrack for your brunch. From acoustic guitar to smooth jazz, our performers will set the perfect mood for a relaxing day with family.

Family-Friendly Activities

At Red Rocks, we know that Mother’s Day is all about spending quality time with the ones you love. That’s why we’ve planned a variety of activities to keep kids of all ages entertained, from face painting to lawn games. Parents can relax and enjoy their brunch while the little ones run around and have fun.

Q&A Session

Q: Do I need to make a reservation for Mother’s Day brunch at Red Rocks?

A: Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation in advance to ensure availability.

Q: Is Red Rocks a family-friendly restaurant?

A: Absolutely! We love welcoming families and kids of all ages to our restaurant, and have plenty of activities and menu options to keep everyone happy.

Q: Can I bring my dog to Red Rocks for brunch?

A: Unfortunately, we do not allow pets on our outdoor dining patio. However, we do offer a separate takeout menu that you can enjoy with your furry friend in one of the nearby parks.

Q: Is there a dress code for Mother’s Day brunch at Red Rocks?

A: We do not have a formal dress code, but we recommend dressing comfortably and appropriately for the outdoor setting. Sunscreen and hats are always a good idea!

Q: Are there vegetarian or gluten-free options on the Mother’s Day brunch menu?

A: Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and gluten-free options on our brunch menu. Please let your server know about any dietary restrictions when placing your order.
