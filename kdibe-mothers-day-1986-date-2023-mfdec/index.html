The History of Mother’s Day

Mother’s Day has been celebrated for over a century. It was first celebrated in the United States in 1908, when Anna Jarvis held a memorial for her mother who had passed away. She wanted to honor her mother and all mothers by creating a day dedicated to them. Over the years, Mother’s Day has become a worldwide celebration, where people of all cultures and backgrounds come together to celebrate the most important woman in their lives.
