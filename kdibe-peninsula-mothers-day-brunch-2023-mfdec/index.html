When is Mother’s Day 2023?

Mother’s Day 2023 falls on Sunday, May 14th. This is the perfect day to appreciate the hard work and love that our mothers put into raising us. One great way to show your appreciation is by treating your mom to a delicious and relaxing brunch.

What is Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch?

Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch is an annual event held in the beautiful Peninsula area. It is a celebration of motherhood, family, and love. Guests can enjoy a wide variety of delicious food, including brunch favorites like pancakes, waffles, bacon, and eggs. There are also vegetarian and gluten-free options available.

Where is Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch held?

The location of Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch changes every year. In 2023, it will be held at the Peninsula Country Club, which is known for its stunning views and serene atmosphere. This location is perfect for a relaxing brunch with Mom.

What can guests expect at Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch?

Guests can expect a warm and welcoming atmosphere, delicious food, and great company. There will be live music, activities for kids, and a photo booth to capture memories with Mom. The event is designed for families to enjoy quality time together, while also celebrating the special bond between a mother and her children.

The Food is Delicious

The food at Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch is always delicious. You can indulge in all your favorite brunch foods, as well as some new and exciting dishes. There are also vegetarian and gluten-free options available, so everyone can enjoy the meal.

The Atmosphere is Relaxing

It’s a Fun Family Activity

Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch is designed for families to enjoy quality time together. There are activities for kids, live music, and a photo booth to capture memories. It’s a fun and meaningful way to spend Mother’s Day with your loved ones.

Tips for Making the Most of Peninsula Mother’s Day Brunch

Make a Reservation
